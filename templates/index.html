Horse Health Prediction

Welcome to our Flask-based Horse Health Prediction App!

Using Machine learning, our application utilizes a Kaggle dataset to predict the outcomes of horses' health conditions. By analyzing various factors, we provide insights into whether a horse is likely to live, face euthanasia, or unfortunately, succumb to its health challenges. This user-friendly tool aims to assist veterinarians, horse owners, and enthusiasts in making informed decisions about the well-being of these magnificent animals. Explore the future of equine health with our predictive model!
